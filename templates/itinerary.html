💡 Travel Tips for {{ itinerary.location }}

{% if itinerary.location == "Goa" %}
  • Best Time: November to May (avoid monsoon)
  • Transport: Rent scooters or use local taxis
  • Currency: Carry some cash for small purchases
  • Safety: Be cautious with valuables on crowded beaches
{% elif itinerary.location == "Kerala" %}
  • Best Time: September to March
  • Transport: Houseboats, local buses, or rented cars
  • Cuisine: Try appam, fish curry, and avial
  • Weather: Can be humid, carry light cotton clothing
{% elif itinerary.location == "Rajasthan" %}
  • Best Time: October to March
  • Transport: Trains, buses, or self-drive
  • Culture: Respect local customs and dress modestly
  • Bargaining: Expected in markets, start with half the asking price
{% elif itinerary.location == "Himachal Pradesh" %}
  • Best Time: March to June, September to November
  • Altitude: Acclimatize to avoid altitude sickness
  • Transport: Toy train from Kalka to Shimla is recommended
  • Weather: Carry warm clothes, temperatures drop at night
{% elif itinerary.location == "Uttarakhand" %}
  • Best Time: March to June, September to November
  • Spirituality: Maintain silence at religious sites
  • Activities: Yoga, meditation, and nature walks
  • Transport: Shared jeeps and local buses
{% elif itinerary.location == "Mumbai" %}
  • Best Time: October to May
  • Transport: Local trains, taxis, or metro
  • Street Food: Try pav bhaji, vada pav, and bhel puri
  • Timing: Avoid peak hours for local trains
{% elif itinerary.location == "Delhi" %}
  • Best Time: October to March
  • Transport: Metro, taxis, or app-based cabs
  • Monuments: Book tickets online to avoid queues
  • Water: Drink only bottled or filtered water
{% elif itinerary.location == "Agra" %}
  • Best Time: October to March
  • Taj Mahal: Visit at sunrise for best experience
  • Tickets: Book online in advance
  • Transport: Day trip from Delhi is convenient
{% elif itinerary.location == "Varanasi" %}
  • Best Time: October to March
  • Ganges: Early morning boat rides are magical
  • Culture: Respect local customs and photography rules
  • Transport: Cycle rickshaws or walk in old city
{% elif itinerary.location == "Jaipur" %}
  • Best Time: October to March
  • Amber Fort: Visit early morning, elephant ride optional
  • Shopping: Johari Bazaar for jewelry and textiles
  • Transport: Auto-rickshaws or taxis
{% elif itinerary.location == "Udaipur" %}
  • Best Time: October to March
  • Lake Cruise: Evening cruise on Lake Pichola
  • Architecture: Known as Venice of East
  • Transport: Walking distance in old city
{% elif itinerary.location == "Pondicherry" %}
  • Best Time: November to March
  • Transport: Bicycles or auto-rickshaws
  • Cuisine: French bakeries and South Indian food
  • Colonial: French Quarter has European architecture
{% elif itinerary.location == "Shimla" %}
  • Best Time: March to June, December to February
  • Mall Road: Main shopping and dining area
  • Transport: Walking or horse rides
  • Weather: Can be foggy, carry warm clothes
{% elif itinerary.location == "Manali" %}
  • Best Time: March to June
  • Rohtang: Requires special permit
  • Activities: Paragliding, rafting, trekking
  • Transport: Buses from Delhi, then local taxis
{% elif itinerary.location == "Leh Ladakh" %}
  • Best Time: June to September
  • Altitude: Acclimatize properly to avoid AMS
  • Transport: Flight to Leh, then SUVs
  • Weather: Extreme temperatures, carry warm gear
{% elif itinerary.location == "Kashmir" %}
  • Best Time: April to October
  • Houseboats: Stay on Dal Lake for authentic experience
  • Gardens: Mughal gardens are at their best in spring
  • Transport: Shikaras on Dal Lake
{% elif itinerary.location == "Bangalore" %}
  • Best Time: October to March
  • Transport: Metro, buses, or app-based cabs
  • Cuisine: Try filter coffee and South Indian food
  • Weather: Pleasant climate year-round
{% elif itinerary.location == "Chennai" %}
  • Best Time: December to February
  • Transport: Local trains, auto-rickshaws
  • Cuisine: Dosas, idlis, and filter coffee
  • Culture: Rich music and dance heritage
{% elif itinerary.location == "Kolkata" %}
  • Best Time: October to March
  • Transport: Metro, taxis, or hand-pulled rickshaws
  • Cuisine: Street food and Bengali sweets
  • Culture: Durga Puja and literary heritage
{% elif itinerary.location == "Ayodhya" %}
  • Best Time: October to March
  • Temple: Ram Janmabhoomi Temple visit
  • Transport: Trains from major cities
  • Culture: Deep spiritual significance
{% else %}
  • Best Time: Check local weather conditions
  • Transport: Research local transportation options
  • Culture: Respect local customs and traditions
